
==== get Tag ====

The get tag Resolves to the string representation of the value of an XPath expression


^ Attribute      ^ Kind   ^ Required    ^ Purpose          ^
|**select**  |string  |yes  |An XPath expression  |

=== Full Form ===

<**c:get**  **select**="" >
