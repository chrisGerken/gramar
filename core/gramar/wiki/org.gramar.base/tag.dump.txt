
==== dump Tag ====

The dump tag Writes a string representation of the specified node and its children.


^ Attribute      ^ Kind   ^ Required    ^ Purpose          ^
|**select**  |node  |yes  |The node whose string representation is to be written.  |

=== Full Form ===

<**c:dump**  **select**="" >
